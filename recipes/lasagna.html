Gluten-free Lasagna

  • 12 gluten-free lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups gluten-free marinara sauce
  • 500g ground beef or turkey (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  1. Cook the noodles: Boil the gluten-free lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce: In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook the ground meat until browned. Add marinara sauce, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix the cheese filling: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella, and half the Parmesan. Stir until smooth.
  4. Layer the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then a layer of the cheese mixture, and top with meat sauce. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce.
  5. Top with cheese: Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  6. Bake: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Serve: Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
